Movie Reviews

Rekhachithram

Directed by Jofin T. Chacko, this mystery crime thriller stars Asif Ali and Anaswara Rajan. The plot revolves around an investigation linked to the 1985 film 'Kathodu Kathoram.' The film has been praised for its unique backdrop and engaging narrative.

Bromance

This comedy-drama explores the deep bond between two childhood friends as they navigate life's challenges together. Critics appreciated its heartfelt storytelling and the chemistry between the lead actors.

Dragon

The film follows Raghavan, affectionately known as "Dragon," portrayed by Pradeep Ranganathan, a carefree college student with 48 arrears who embarks on a journey of self-discovery and redemption.
